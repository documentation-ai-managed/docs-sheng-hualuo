## Overview

Adding your website lets you monitor performance, fix issues, and improve visibility on Bing.

## Steps

1. Sign in at `https://www.bing.com/webmasters`
2. Click "Add a Site"
3. Enter your website URL and click "Add"
4. Verify ownership via file upload, meta tag, or DNS record

Once verified, access insights and optimization tools immediately.
