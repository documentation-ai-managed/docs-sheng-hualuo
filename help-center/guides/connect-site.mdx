## Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to `https://www.bing.com/webmasters` and sign in with Microsoft, Google, or Facebook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your site">
    Click "Add a Site" and enter your domain.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a verification method">
    Pick file upload, meta tag, or DNS.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete verification">
    Follow on-screen instructions, then click "Verify" to finish.
  </Step>
</Steps>
