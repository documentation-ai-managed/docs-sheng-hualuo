## Overview

Sheng Hualuo provides free webmaster tools powered by AI-driven search. You use it to add your websites to the index, monitor performance, and discover traffic opportunities. Register your site to access analytics, optimization settings, and insights that improve visibility.

The platform simplifies site management with intuitive dashboards and AI recommendations. Focus on key metrics like index coverage, search performance, and crawl errors to enhance your online presence.

Sheng Hualuo supports all website types, including static sites, SPAs, and dynamic apps.

## Quick Start

Get your site verified and active in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create a free account at https://dashboard.example.com/register.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Property" icon="plus">
    Enter your domain and select verification method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Site" icon="check-circle">
    Choose from multiple methods below.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Sitemap" icon="upload">
    Upload your sitemap.xml for faster indexing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Verification Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Meta Tag" icon="code">
    Add this to your site's `<head>` section:

    ````html
    <meta name="sheng-hualuo-site-verification" content="your-verification-code-here">
    ````

    Replace `your-verification-code-here` with the code from your dashboard.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="HTML File" icon="file">
    Download the verification file and upload to your root directory:

    ````html
    <!-- sheng-hualuo-verification-file.html -->
    <html>
    <head><title>Sheng Hualuo Verification</title></head>
    <body>your-verification-code-here</body>
    </html>
    ````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="DNS TXT" icon="globe">
    Add a TXT record to your DNS:

    ````bash
    sheng-hualuo-site-verification=your-verification-code-here
    ````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
